Privacy Policy for PalPair

Introduction

Welcome to PalPair, a random video chat app that allows users to meet new people and chat via video. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of personal information we collect, how we use it, and the steps we take to protect your privacy.

Information We Collect

When you register on PalPair, we collect the following information:

  • Email Address: Used for account registration and login.
  • Password: Used to secure your account.
  • Age: To ensure you meet the required age for using our service.
  • Location: This information helps to connect you with users from your region.
  • Gender: For matchmaking and user experience enhancement.

All information is stored securely in our database. We do not require email verification for account registration.

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used to provide and improve our services, specifically to:

  • Allow you to create and manage your account.
  • Connect you with other users for video chats.
  • Enhance your experience based on location, age, and gender preferences.
  • Send you notifications related to your account and app activities.

Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect the personal information you provide to us from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, please note that no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your data.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information stored with us. If you would like to request any changes to your account or have your data deleted, please contact us directly through the contact details provided below.

Sharing Your Information

We do not share your personal information with third parties except as required by law or to enhance the functionality of our services (such as through third-party analytics tools). We will never sell or rent your personal data to any third party.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make changes, we will update this page and notify you through the app or via email. Please review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.
